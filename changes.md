**A Peaceful Path with Hospice Care**

Hospice care begins with the understanding that every person’s experience is unique. Rather than focusing on cure, hospice shifts attention toward comfort, ease, and the needs of the individual. Our intention is to create a supportive environment where families can be present with one another and share meaningful moments.

It is common to assume that hospice means stepping away from care, when in reality it brings additional layers of support. Hospice provides physical comfort, emotional guidance, and spiritual companionship for both the individual and their loved ones. Our team offers steady support, helping to create a sense of calm and a more peaceful environment throughout this transition. Care continues beyond a loved one’s passing through grief counseling and compassionate guidance for those adjusting to life after loss.

## **Our Process**

**Compassionate Hospice Support for Families in Southern California**

Whether you’re seeking support for yourself or someone you love, we understand how important sensitivity and understanding are in this moment. We listen closely, honor personal wishes, and provide compassionate care that helps ease this transition with the utmost respect, dignity, and reverence for the natural course of life.

**1\. Meet With Our Care Coordinator**
We begin with a warm, supportive conversation to understand the needs of the person receiving care, answer any questions you may have, and explain how hospice can provide comfort during this transition. There is never any obligation to enroll. We are simply here to guide and support you.

**Frequently Asked Questions:**

**What is hospice care?**
Hospice focuses on comfort, symptom relief, and quality of life when a cure is no longer possible.
**Is hospice covered by Medicare?**
Yes. Hospice is fully covered by Medicare, Medi-Cal, and most private insurance plans.
**Can hospice be provided at home?**
Absolutely. Our team provides complete hospice services in the comfort of your home.
**What support do families receive?**
We provide hands-on RN/LVN nursing care, medical social work support, respite services, bereavement counseling, and emotional and spiritual care. Our team also offers physical, occupational, and speech therapies to enhance comfort and quality of life. On-call support is available 24/7, and in-home visits are scheduled based on each patient’s needs and individualized care plan.
**How quickly can care begin?**
Most families can start services the same day or within 24 hours.
